about this trip:

Go on a local adventure

Head off the beaten path and discover local life in Essaouira. Sample pastries and fresh grilled fish at the market place, and visit local artisans at work.

Get a taste of Marrakech

Sample Moroccan delicacies in the souks on this tasty tour and become a spice guru after a visit to a local spice market.

Check out the Berber trail

Morocco's original inhabitants, the Berber people live their lives relatively unchanged. Meet them on an "off-road" adventure, which takes you into the Atlas Mountains.

Try your hand at cooking

Take a cooking lesson at a Kasbah, where a traditional chef will share the secrets of Moroccan cooking, its history, and tradition.
